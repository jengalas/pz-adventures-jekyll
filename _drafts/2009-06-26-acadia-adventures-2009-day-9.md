Breakfast at gazebo, cruise ship and triangle mark, ride from Bubble Pond to Jordan Pond House, back to Bubble Pond and then around Eagle Lake, supper at Havana, puzzle.

3 seed bagel with cream cheese, white peony tea; croissant, coffee (R) at Morning Glory; curried chicken salad sandwich, chips, pickle, popovers, blueberry tea, coffee (R) at JPH; carrot-ginger flan with green onion on toasted flour tortilla, vanilla cornbread with carrot cake butter, meat pastel, hanger steak, seafood stew (look up name?), mojitos, Jack Daniels ice cream, tres tres leches w/passionfruit habanero sauce.
