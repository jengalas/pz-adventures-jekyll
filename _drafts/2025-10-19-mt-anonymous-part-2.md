I went up and over Mt. Anonymous, not quite to the reservoir on the other side (just didn’t want to go all the way down and then have to come back up again) but I did see it through the trees.  At that point, the road branched off going up and over the ridge (at least, I assume that’s the trail we were talking about).  I followed it for a while, until I seemed to be heading too far away for the amount of time I had left.  Rather than retracing my steps, at that point I just bushwhacked a bit by following a dry stream bed down the hill and then coming across an old road grade that led me right to Ore Mine Road.  From there, I just ran along Ore Mine Road and back up to the paved path around the lake, and then back to the car.
