## The Reward

Aaron, Rich and I met the guys from GMBC at the Elks Club around 9:15 AM and chatted for a while as we waited for all the teams to arrive. We made the decision to begin eating while waiting for certain stragglers to appear.

Breakfast was a great deal at only $5.00 for all you could eat of homemade French toast, pancakes, fried potatoes and a wonderful cheesy potato casserole, scrambled eggs, bacon and sausage, and plenty of other goodies to munch on and to drink. I wholeheartedly recommend it!

{% include gallery.html gallery_id="gallery1" class="" %}

After clearing our plates, we played our hands one card at a time (for effect, of course). One of the younger cachers pulled the fifth card to complete our hands ... and it was the Ace of Spades! Originally tricked by the triple Queens played by Team Ferret, our fearless leader from GMBC gave the stack of dollar coins to that team for their full house. But wait—Team Zhanna and Rich in NEPA had a full house with three Aces! The breakfast reimbursement then was passed to its rightful winners. I think we all had a great time. I'm hoping GMBC will host another similar event soon!
