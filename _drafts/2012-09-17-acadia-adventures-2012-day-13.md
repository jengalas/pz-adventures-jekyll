Cafe This Way. Pothole (Pot Hole, Potholes) Trail!  Town Hill Bistro (lavender creme brulee! Is this what summoned the moose?!), drive through Park Loop Road with stop at Thunder Hole and many deer. MOOSE!!!!!!

My husband and I did (finally!) see a moose in Bar Harbor on September 17 at around 7:45pm, in the parking lot of the Cromwell Harbor Motel.  My husband has been visiting MDI annually for 40 years and had never seen a moose until Monday night.  It was a thrilling experience.  We had just taken a final drive along the Park Loop Road as is our custom on the last night of our vacation.  While driving back into town, I noticed a set of moose-like legs moving from the road shoulder into the parking lot of the Cromwell Harbor Motel.  I assumed it was a deer, as we had just seen four deer on our drive through the parkâ€”but as we passed I turned my head to check, just to be sure.  Immediately I saw the unmistakable antlers of a moose.  I exclaimed to my husband to turn the car around, because I had just seen a moose.  We pulled into the parking lot of the motel and had to swing around to the back before I spotted him again, in a strip of grass bordering the lot.  The bull moose was calmly pulling at the lowest tree branches and poking around in the shrubs.  He looked at us several times but did not seem distressed.  We watched him for more than five minutes until he slipped into the brush at the rear of the property, perhaps headed toward Strawberry Hill or the ballfield.  We still wonder whether anyone else saw the moose that night.
