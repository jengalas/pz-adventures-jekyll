## The Official Invitation

> **Geocachers, welcome to an "Event Cache by Green Mountain Bike Club."**
>
>This is the plan. First there is only room for 12 cachers (or 12 teams).
- This event will be in game lands #35. This is in the Hallstead/Great Bend area in PA.
- The caching starts at the entrance to Rose Valley.
- Park here: **N 41° 57.520' W 075° 39.550'**
>
> This is an event cache. The event will be a poker game. Get yourself a book and read up on the hands of poker. The game consists of four (4) different caches. Each cache contains 12 playing cards. You (or your team) are to go to each cache and take one card (only ONE) from each cache. Once you have visited the cache, you cannot return to the cache and change the card. (If you are a team, the entire team must visit each cache. No splitting the chores.) You will have four cards. I have randomly taken four cards from the deck. (Each cache will have only 12 cards).
> 
> At the "poker game" (the get together) at the Elk's Club on February 15, at 9:30 AM, your cards will be placed face-up. The cards that I have taken will be placed face down. A volunteer from GMBC will flip one of the 4 cards. This card will be used by all players as their hole card. No wild cards, just 5-card stud poker. Best hand wins.
>
> "Wins what?" you ask. At the get together on Sunday morning before the all-you-can-eat-breakfast, we will play the hand at 9:30 a.m. at the Elk’s Club. The winner wins breakfast for two and bragging rights for the event. If you were looking for a bigger prize, buy a lottery ticket on your way to caching at the Sunoco (our GMBC meeting place) home of the Green Mountain Coffee, thus the name Green Mountain Bike Club. (GMBC does not want any cacher to miss their Sunday morning worship service. If you need to play your hand via the internet because you can’t attend breakfast, e-mail me a picture of your hand and it will be played for you. You will win bragging rights, but the breakfast will go to the highest attendee.)
>
> I will send the coordinates to the 4 caches to the first 12 players that send an e-mail to GMBC. You will have from now until February 13th to retrieve your cards. GMBC will be collecting the cache containers on Saturday, February 14, and the breakfast is Sunday, February 15, at the Elk’s Club in Kirkwood, NY. (**N 42° 04.012' W 075° 48.198'**)
